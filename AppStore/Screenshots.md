## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- Primary: iOS Blue (#007AFF)
- Accent: Whisper Orange (#FF6B35)
- Background: Pure White (#FFFFFF) / Dark Gray (#1C1C1E)
- Text: System Black / White

### Typography
- Headlines: SF Pro Display Bold, 48pt
- Body: SF Pro Text Regular, 24pt
- Captions: SF Pro Text Medium, 18pt

### Common Elements
- WhisperBoard logo in corner
- Device frame (iPhone 15 Pro recommended)
- Subtle shadow under device
- Clean, minimal aesthetic

## Tools for Creation

1. **Figma** - Design mockups (free)
2. **Screenshot Maker** (iOS app) - Generate device frames
3. **AppLaunchpad** - Screenshot templates
4. **Previewed** - 3D device mockups
5. **DaVinci Resolve** - Video editing (free)

## Export Settings
- Format: PNG (screenshots)
- Color Space: sRGB
- No transparency (fill background)
- No status bar (clean screenshots)
